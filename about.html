Recycle. Reinvent. Revamp.

Infuse your clothes with fresh purpose as you uncover innovative ways to breathe new life into your wardrobe, transforming pieces from the forgotten corners into statement ensembles that reflect your unique style and commitment to sustainability.

Our Mission

Upcycling reduces the demand for new clothing production, which is a core driver of the fast fashion industry. By repurposing and modifying existing garments, you actively participate in decreasing the market pressure for constant production and consumption of new clothes.

Our Founders.

Prisha Basak

Founder

Born and raised in New Delhi, Basak’s journey in fashion began at the young age of 8, having watched her grandmother sew on her old sewing machine. Inspired by the magic that thread and cloth together could create it became evident that this was what she wanted to do with her life.
