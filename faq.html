Answers to some questions you might have



A thrift store, often called a secondhand store or a thrift shop, is a retail establishment that offers used, donated, or pre-owned goods at cheaper costs than those offered by standard retail establishments. Clothing, footwear, accessories, furniture, household goods, technology, books, toys, and other items can be included in this category. Charity groups, non-profits, or for-profit companies frequently run thrift stores.
