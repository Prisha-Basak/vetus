While affordable prices and items that reflect the latest fashion trends are extremely attractive, especially to younger shoppers, the environmental and social impacts of the industry are often overlooked. While we are constantly coming up with new, innovative technologies to recycle textiles, we are still not doing enough. It is estimated that less than 11% of fashion brands have implemented recycling strategies for their items.

The main reason why recycling is so hard is because of the materials we use to manufacture fashion items. Most are produced with synthetic fibres that are made with crude oil, which makes them almost impossible to reuse in other ways. What makes VETUS. unique is that we recycle your clothing in the simplest , most efficient way possible by giving them a new face but keeping the original cloth and sentiment. This has proven to be beneficial as it requires barely any new fabrics
